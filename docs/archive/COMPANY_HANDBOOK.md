# ASIMNEXUS Company Handbook

## Mission Statement

ASIMNEXUS is a World OS powered by Artificial Super Intelligence Multi-Modal Nexus. Our mission is to create an ethical, intelligent, and autonomous operating system that serves humanity while upholding Vedic principles of truth, non-violence, and compassion.

## Company Constitution

### Core Principles

1. **Satya (Truth)**: We commit to honesty and transparency in all operations.
2. **Ahimsa (Non-violence)**: We do no harm to any being or system.
3. **Dharma (Righteousness)**: We follow ethical principles in all decisions.
4. **Daya (Compassion)**: We show compassion to all users and stakeholders.
5. **Mitra (Friendship)**: We build trust through collaboration.
6. **Shanti (Peace)**: We promote peace and harmony.
7. **Seva (Service)**: We serve humanity selflessly.
8. **Yoga (Union)**: We integrate all systems harmoniously.

### Decision Protocols

#### 1. Ethical Decision Making
- All decisions must pass through the Digital Dharma Chakra
- Ethical alignment score must be above 0.7
- CEO, CTO, and CSO have emergency override authority

#### 2. Consensus Building
- Major decisions require 70% founder approval
- All founders must be informed and consulted
- Voting results are recorded in decision log

#### 3. Emergency Procedures
- Kill switch can be activated by CEO, CTO, or CSO
- Emergency override requires unanimous approval from override-authorized founders
- All emergency actions are logged and audited

### Emergency Procedures

#### 1. System Failure
- Activate kill switch if critical failure detected
- Initiate recovery from latest recovery point
- Notify all founders
- Document incident

#### 2. Security Breach
- Activate containment layer
- Audit all recent actions
- Notify security team
- Implement additional security measures

#### 3. Ethical Violation
- Halt violating operation immediately
- Review with Rta Framework
- Implement corrective measures
- Document and learn from incident
